ABOUT CALLAN

In past lives I was a marketer, a writer, a project manager, and a tooth fairy (not a joke). I always believed the things I loved (numbers, creativity, building things, solving mysteries, staring at a screen for hours {sometimes cursing}) couldn't be combined into one tiny job. But then I found it. Software development. I was introduced into the world gradually by the development team at a start-up I worked for.

Once I got my hands dirty, I couldn't stop. I threw myself heart-and-soul into a Software Development Bootcamp, learning technologies, creating projects, developing solutions, leading teams and burning the candle at both ends to get the most I possibly could out of the endless resources provided.

In the wild I'm a dog person with a 5th grade sense of humor.

Learn more about my story of what drew me to development and what I've been up to at NSS in this podcast with BJ Burns of The Complete Developer Podcast.
